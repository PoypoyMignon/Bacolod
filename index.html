Explore | the Philippines

Bacolod City in the Philippines boasts a rich history and cultural heritage, famous for its delicious "chicken inasal" cuisine. The city is home to peaceful parks like the Bacolod People's Park and the Negros Forest Park, as well as lively festivals like the MassKara Festival. With welcoming locals, warm hospitality, and a tropical climate, Bacolod is a must-visit destination for those seeking a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty.
